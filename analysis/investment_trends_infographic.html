Visibility gap is a market signal

With only 45% coverage, the dataset suggests the quantum capital map is still opaque. That makes disclosed leaders more visible, but also means stealth and underreported companies may be missing from the top tier.

Funding is not purely hardware-led

The disclosed leaderboard includes software and full-stack names, supporting the view that investors are backing system-layer value, not just device development.

Current trend read: directional, not final

The dataset lacks round timing and many totals, so this infographic is best used to identify concentration and market visibility rather than precise sector-wide capital rankings.
