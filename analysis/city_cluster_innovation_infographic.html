Why Investors Should Care

Clusters explain innovation better because they reveal how capability actually compounds.

Density

Startups matter more when the right researchers, engineers, and operators sit within one hiring and meeting radius.

Specialization

City hubs reveal whether a market skews toward software, hardware, control systems, cryptography, or mixed full-stack development.

Maturity

Corridors reveal whether a hub is still research-heavy or already translating into pilot, production, and revenue-stage activity.

Coordination

A corridor can support suppliers, labs, testbeds, partners, and experienced operators, not just startup counts.

Bottom line: country maps are good for macro orientation. City and corridor maps are better for understanding where quantum innovation is accelerating, commercializing, and compounding.
